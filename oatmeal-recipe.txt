Ingredients:
 - 1 tablespoon peanut butter
 - 1/2 cup milk
 - 1 bag oats
 - 1 mashed banana
 - 1 pinch of cinnamon
 - 1 handful of almonds
 - 1 large egg

Directions:
  1. Heat milk and oats in microwave for 1 minute.
  2. Mix in all the other ingredients into the warm oatmilk.
  3. Heat for another 60-90 seconds.
  4. Enjoy heaven on earth.
